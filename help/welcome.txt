Can't figure out how to chat? Just click near the bottom of the screen, to the left of the send button.
<hr>
Want to stop being a guest? Do /iam yourname. Sadly, you cannot have spaces.
We have four servers! Pick which one you want to use by typing /nexus.
Other things you can do are listed in help! Type /help commands for the entire list.
<hr>
We have dark mode! To turn on dark mode, press Ctrl-Alt-D. To turn it off, press it again.
